# Module: Spells, Artifacts & Hero Skills (M3)

The depth layer. Spells, artifacts, and secondary skills are what make individual heroes feel different and make the mid/late game strategically rich. Without these, every game plays the same.

**Milestone**: M3 — Depth  
**Total Estimate**: ~91 hours
**Exit Criteria**: Heroes cast spells in combat and adventure map; artifacts grant stat bonuses; 28 secondary skills are learnable.
