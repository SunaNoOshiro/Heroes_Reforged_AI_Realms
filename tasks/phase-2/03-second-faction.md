# Module: Second Faction — Necropolis (M3)

Add the second playable faction. Necropolis was chosen for its unique
Necromancy mechanic, which requires sim-level support and stress-tests
the content system. Necropolis is also the most thematically distinct
from Emberwild (the reference faction), providing the best coverage
test for balance assumptions.

**Milestone**: M3 — Depth
**Total Estimate**: ~18 hours
**Exit Criteria**: A Necropolis army can fight and win against
Emberwild; Necromancy raises skeletons after combat.
